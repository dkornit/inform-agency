About Us

At NewspaperTrackSystem, we believe in transparency and accountability in journalism. Our platform helps track which author wrote which article and when, ensuring credibility and trust in the media landscape.

Our Mission

To provide a structured and efficient way for news agencies and independent journalists to manage and track article authorship, ensuring accuracy and accountability.

How It Works

  • Redactors: Every journalist has a profile with their experience and published work.
  • Topics: Articles are categorized by topics for better organization.
  • Newspapers: Each article includes its title, content, publication date, and associated journalists.

Why Choose NewsTrack?

  • Clear authorship tracking – Always know who wrote what.
  • Easy categorization – Find articles quickly based on topics.
  • Collaboration-friendly – Multiple authors can contribute to a single piece.
  • Time-saving – No more manual tracking, everything is automated.
